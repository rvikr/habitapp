# Lagan MVP Social Post Brief

## Asset

Square social post for Lagan, sized for Instagram, LinkedIn, X, and general feed use.

## Approved feature claims used

- AI habit tracker and coach
- AI routine builder
- Smart reminders
- Habit logging and progress tracking
- Streaks, XP, badges, and leaderboard/ranks
- Sleep and step tracking as app-supported habit context
- Free to start
- iOS, Android, and web availability

## Avoided

- Fake ratings, fake review quotes, unsupported download counts, and store badges without final store links.
- Medical, health-outcome, or guaranteed behavior-change claims.

## Production note

The visual uses the local Lagan app icon, the repo's public landing-page positioning, and a deterministic HTML phone mockup so text and brand elements remain crisp for export.
